Origins of Imbolc


    This holiday was origionally the marker for the halfway point between Winter and Spring. The day is supposed to represent a rebirth from the death of Winter. This holiday was also a mean to celebrate the Goddess of rebirth, Brigid. Legends say that Brigid was born with a flame in her head and to acknowledge this, flaming effigies were built in her honor.
